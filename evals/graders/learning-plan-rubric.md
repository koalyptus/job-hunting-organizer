# Learning Plan Rubric v1

**Prompt version:** 1
**Last updated:** 2026-07-10

## Dimensions

Evaluate the learning plan against these dimensions. Each dimension is scored independently.

### 1. grounding

Check these rules:

- **Weak-topic coverage**: Every weak topic from the input is addressed with a dedicated subsection.
- **Profile alignment**: Topics reference skills, technologies, or experience from the candidate's profile.
- **JD relevance**: At least one topic relates to requirements in the job description.
- **No fabrication**: Resources reference real, well-known sources (official docs, established books, reputable blogs). Do not invent URLs.

### 2. quality

- **Specificity**: Each topic has actionable, concrete advice — "read chapter 5 of DDIA" not "study distributed systems".
- **Resource count**: Each topic has 2–4 resources with actionable links or references.
- **Time estimates**: Realistic time estimates per topic (not "spend 5 minutes").
- **Checklist**: Final checklist includes actionable items the candidate can track (4–8 items).

### 3. tone

- **Constructive**: Tone is supportive and encouraging without being patronising.
- **No AI disclosure**: Does not mention being an AI, language model, or assistant.
- **No banned phrases**: Does not use modesty/boast language (e.g. "best", "exceptional", "world-class").
